#  Online Taxpayer Services
The City of Los Angeles has changed our banking institution and no longer requires ACH Credit for payments of $50,000 or more. Customers are now able to use the e-check option when making payments online. Please click the appropriate link below to make your payment online.
Please email us at Finance.ACH@lacity.org for questions regarding ACH transactions or to request the new routing instructions.
