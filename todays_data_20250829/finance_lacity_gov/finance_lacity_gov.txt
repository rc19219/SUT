## STELARR - Tobacco Retailing Training Course
Standardized Tobacco E-Training for Los Angeles Responsible Retailers (STELARR) is available online for business owners and employees. Being aware of tobacco retailing laws can help your business avoid violations, which can result in tobacco permit suspensions and fines.
[For additional information on the STELARR program, please click here.](http://finance21.cityofla.acsitefactory.com/sites/g/files/wph1721/files/2024-10/STELARR%20-%20Public%20Flyer.pdf)
